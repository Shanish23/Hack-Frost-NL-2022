About Us

We are a group of students of Memorial University trying to tackle the recycling problem of glassware and in the process of doing so we are also helping convicts do community service we are located here in St John's NL. We are offering services to the general public to pick up their unwanted glassware

Our goal: Pick up your unwanted glassware

Services We Offer

We are have a range of services and we are available to pick up the glassware on a monthly basis if needed and if required, we could be available within a week. Do all these while getting paid for your trash. We will pay you a minimum of $10 for 100lbs of glassware
